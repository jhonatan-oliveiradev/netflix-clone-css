In Mulan, Hua Mulan (Liu Yifei) is the spirited and determined eldest daughter of an honorable warrior. When the Emperor of China issues a decree that one man from each family must serve in the imperial army, Mulan decides to take the place of her father, who is sick. Assuming the identity of Hua Jun, she disguises herself as a man to fight the invaders that are attacking her nation, proving herself to be a great warrior.
